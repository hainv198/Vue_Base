1. What type of advice is covered by software consultancy?

An experienced software consultancy like Groove Technology helps you make wiser decisions about software and business systems. In software development, practical skills are important. Equally important is the reasoning and planning that sits behind technical activities. For example, we might assist you to determine:

  • What kinds of software and systems align with your business model/plan?
  • Why might you choose one kind of technology or solution over another?
  • What is the best approach to developing and implementing a solution?
  • How to manage business-wide impacts of new systems/technologies?

2. Why is software development consulting valuable?

An objective, seasoned, and external opinion is worthwhile for software development projects of all sizes, in all industries. Technology is a key business driver but it can be expensive.
